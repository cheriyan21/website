Project Overview

This project mainly focuses on entering data and evaluating day to day expenses this was my first web page which i have created in my early times

I have developed a web-based application called Expense Tracker. This app serves as a comprehensive logbook, enabling users to meticulously track their overall financial status by logging expenses and incomes. The application is designed to calculate the net loss or profit based on the entries made. Additionally, it offers the convenience of downloading the financial summary as a PDF, making it easy to keep records or share the financial report with others.
